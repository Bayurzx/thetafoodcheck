  • What is Foodcheck AI?

    Foodcheck AI is an innovative application that helps users make informed choices about their food items by using artificial intelligence to analyze ingredients and compare them with the user's health data.

  • What kind of health data does Foodcheck AI use?

    Foodcheck AI uses a variety of health data provided by the user, including dietary restrictions, allergies, medical conditions, and nutritional preferences. This data is used to generate personalized insights and recommendations.

  • How does Foodcheck AI work?

    Foodcheck AI uses advanced algorithms to scan and analyze the ingredients list of food and drug products. It then compares this information with the user’s health profile, providing tailored warnings, advice, recommendations, and food facts in real-time.

  • Who can benefit from using Foodcheck AI?

    Anyone who wants to make health-conscious decisions about their food and drug intake can benefit from using Foodcheck AI. It's especially useful for individuals with specific dietary needs, allergies, or health conditions that require careful monitoring of food ingredients.

  • Can Foodcheck AI help with drug interactions?

    Yes, Foodcheck AI can analyze drug ingredients and provide information on potential interactions based on your health profile. This helps you make informed decisions about over-the-counter and prescription medications.

  • How do I get started with Foodcheck AI?

    Getting started with Foodcheck AI is easy! Simply create an account, and input your health data. Once set up, you can start scanning food and drug products to receive personalized insights and recommendations.
